Connect WhatsApp Business
Official Meta Embedded Signup · 4 steps
We use Meta's official Embedded Signup process. You only need your business Facebook account and the phone number you want to use for WhatsApp Business.
1
Authenticate with Facebook — Log in with your Meta business account.
2
Select or create your WABA — Use an existing account or create a new one.
3
Verify your phone number — Receive verification via SMS or call.
4
Add payment method — Meta bills messages directly to your account.
Your WhatsApp Business assets always belong to you. Axarkia only manages access to send messages on your behalf using whatsapp_business_management and whatsapp_business_messaging permissions.
